📖 Prehistoric World Explained

Learn about Earth during dinosaur times!

🌟 A Different World

When dinosaurs lived, Earth was very different from today! The continents were in different places, the climate was warmer, and plants and animals were unlike anything we see now.

Let's explore what the world was like during the age of dinosaurs!

💡 Fun Fact

All the continents were once connected in one giant supercontinent called Pangea!

🗺️ The Continents

During the time of dinosaurs, the continents were arranged differently:

  • Pangea - All continents connected together
  • Breaking apart - Slowly separated over millions of years
  • Climate zones - Different regions had different climates
  • Land bridges - Dinosaurs could walk between continents

🌡️ The Climate

The climate during dinosaur times was much warmer and wetter than today:

  • No ice caps - The poles had no ice
  • Warmer everywhere - Even the poles were warm
  • More rain - Lots of rainfall everywhere
  • High humidity - Air was very moist

🌿 Plants and Forests

Plants grew huge because of the warm, wet climate:

  • Giant ferns - Ferns as tall as trees
  • Cycads - Palm-like plants everywhere
  • Conifers - Evergreen trees in forests
  • Flowering plants - Appeared later in the Cretaceous

🌊 Oceans and Seas

The oceans were different during dinosaur times:

  • Higher sea levels - Oceans covered more land
  • Shallow seas - Many inland seas existed
  • Marine reptiles - Huge reptiles lived in oceans
  • Warm water - Oceans were warmer than today

💡 Fun Fact

The air had more oxygen than today, which helped dinosaurs grow so large!
