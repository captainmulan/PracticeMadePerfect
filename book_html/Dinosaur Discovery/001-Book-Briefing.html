Dinosaur Discovery

A Journey Through Prehistoric Times!

📖 About This Book

Welcome to Dinosaur Discovery! This interactive ebook will take you on an amazing journey through prehistoric times to learn about dinosaurs, fossils, and the ancient world they lived in.

You'll meet famous dinosaurs, dig for fossils, rotate dinosaur skeletons, and even compete in quizzes against Dr. Rex, a famous paleontologist!

📚 Book Details

  • Target Age: 7-8 years old
  • Total Chapters: 41 chapters
  • Format: Interactive HTML ebook
  • Features: Rotate activities, dig for fossils, matching games, drag-drop quizzes, and Olympic-style podium celebrations!

🎯 What You'll Learn

  • Different dinosaur eras and periods
  • Famous dinosaurs and their characteristics
  • How fossils are formed and discovered
  • What caused dinosaur extinction
  • Prehistoric plants and environments
  • Herbivores vs. Carnivores

🎮 How to Use This Book

  • Start with the Index to see all chapters
  • Choose your character before starting quizzes
  • Interact with dinosaurs by rotating skeletons
  • Dig for fossils in special activity chapters
  • Complete quizzes after each chapter
  • Win medals on the podium after each quiz!
