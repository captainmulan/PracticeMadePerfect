📖 Famous Dinosaurs Explained

Learn about the most well-known dinosaurs!

🌟 Why Are Some Dinosaurs Famous?

Some dinosaurs became super famous because they were discovered early, appeared in movies, or had amazing features that made them stand out!

Let's learn about some of the most famous dinosaurs that everyone knows!

💡 Fun Fact

The first dinosaur ever named was Megalosaurus in 1824!

🦖 Tyrannosaurus Rex

T-Rex is the most famous dinosaur of all! It lived during the Cretaceous period and was one of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs.

  • Size - Up to 12 meters long and 4 meters tall
  • Bite - Had the strongest bite of any land animal
  • Arms - Had tiny arms but very powerful
  • Period - Lived 68-66 million years ago

🦕 Triceratops

Triceratops is famous for its three horns and large frill. It was a plant-eater that lived during the Cretaceous period.

  • Horns - Two long horns above eyes, one on nose
  • Frill - Large bony shield around neck
  • Beak - Had a parrot-like beak for cutting plants
  • Size - Up to 9 meters long

🦕 Stegosaurus

Stegosaurus is famous for the plates along its back and the spikes on its tail. It lived during the Jurassic period.

  • Plates - 17 large bony plates along back
  • Tail spikes - Four sharp spikes on tail for defense
  • Brain - Had a very small brain (walnut-sized)
  • Size - Up to 9 meters long

🦕 Brachiosaurus

Brachiosaurus was one of the largest dinosaurs, famous for its super long neck and huge size!

  • Neck - Could reach 15 meters high
  • Size - Up to 26 meters long
  • Weight - Weighed as much as 15 elephants
  • Period - Lived during the Jurassic

💡 Fun Fact

Brachiosaurus could eat leaves from trees that no other dinosaur could reach!
