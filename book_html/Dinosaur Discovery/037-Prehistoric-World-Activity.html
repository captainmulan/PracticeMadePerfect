Tap on the Earth above to learn about the prehistoric world! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about Earth during the time of dinosaurs.

The Earth was very different when dinosaurs lived. Let's discover how it looked and what it was like!

🌋 The Prehistoric Earth

  • All continents were connected
  • Climate was warmer and wetter
  • Plants grew very large
  • Oceans covered more land
  • Volcanoes were very active
