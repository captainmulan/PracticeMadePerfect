Tap on the dinosaur skeleton above to rotate it! Each time you rotate it, you'll learn a new fun fact about dinosaurs.

Dinosaurs were amazing creatures that lived millions of years ago. Let's discover what made them so special!

🦕 Quick Facts

  • Dinosaurs lived for over 160 million years
  • The word "dinosaur" means "terrible lizard"
  • Some dinosaurs had feathers
  • Birds are actually modern dinosaurs!
  • Dinosaurs lived on every continent
