🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the dinosaur egg above to hatch it! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about dinosaur eggs.

Dinosaurs laid eggs just like modern reptiles and birds. Let's discover what we know about dinosaur eggs!

🦕 Dinosaur Egg Facts

  • Dinosaurs laid eggs in nests
  • Some eggs were as big as footballs
  • Parents often protected their nests
  • Eggs had hard shells like bird eggs
  • Some dinosaurs built nests together
