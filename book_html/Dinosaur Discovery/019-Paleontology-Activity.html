🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the microscope above to learn about paleontology! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about the science of studying fossils.

Paleontology is the study of ancient life through fossils. Paleontologists are like detectives who solve mysteries about the past!

🔍 What Paleontologists Do

  • Search for fossils in the field
  • Carefully excavate fossils
  • Clean and prepare fossils
  • Study and analyze fossils
  • Reconstruct ancient animals
