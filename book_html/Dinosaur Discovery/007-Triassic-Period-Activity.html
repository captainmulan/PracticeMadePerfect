Tap on the dinosaur above to rotate it! Each time you rotate it, you'll learn a new fun fact about the Triassic Period.

The Triassic was the first period of the dinosaur age. Let's discover what made it special!

🌍 Triassic World

  • All continents were joined as Pangea
  • Climate was hot and dry
  • First dinosaurs appeared
  • Early mammals lived alongside dinosaurs
  • Pterosaurs first took to the skies
