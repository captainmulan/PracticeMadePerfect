🪐 What is Solar System Activity

Tap the planets to learn about them!

🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the Sun or any planet in the solar system above to learn fun facts about them! Watch them orbit around the Sun.

Our solar system has 8 planets that all orbit around the Sun. Each planet is unique and special in its own way!

🌟 Meet Our Solar System Family!

  • Sun ☀️ - Our star, the center of our solar system
  • Mercury 🪨 - The smallest and closest to the Sun
  • Venus 🔥 - The hottest planet
  • Earth 🌍 - Our home planet
  • Mars 🔴 - The red planet
  • Jupiter 🟠 - The biggest planet
  • Saturn 💍 - Famous for its beautiful rings
  • Uranus 🔵 - An ice giant that rolls on its side
  • Neptune 🔷 - The windiest planet

💡 Challenge

Try tapping on all 8 planets and the Sun to learn all the facts!
