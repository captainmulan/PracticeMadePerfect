🎮 How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys, WASD, or Touch Buttons to move your spaceship
  • Avoid falling asteroids and meteors (-20 health each hit)
  • Collect power-ups to restore health (+25 health)
  • Survive as long as possible - waves get harder!
  • Score points for every second you survive
  • Game over when health reaches 0%
