🌟 What is Earth?

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only place we know of that has life! It's the perfect distance from the Sun - not too hot and not too cold.

Earth is often called the "Blue Planet" because 71% of its surface is covered in water. It's also the only planet not named after a god!

💧 Water and Life

Earth is the only planet with liquid water on its surface. Water is essential for life - all living things need it to survive!

Earth also has the right atmosphere with oxygen, which humans and animals need to breathe. Plants use carbon dioxide and make oxygen for us!

💡 Fun Fact

Earth is the only planet where fire can burn!

📏 How Big is Earth?

Earth is 12,742 km across. It's not the biggest planet (Jupiter is much bigger), but it's the perfect size for us!

🌍 Layers of Earth

Earth has different layers, just like an onion:

  • Crust - The solid surface we live on
  • Mantle - Hot, semi-solid layer beneath the crust
  • Outer Core - Liquid metal (mostly iron)
  • Inner Core - Solid metal, super hot!

🌙 The Moon

Earth has one moon! It's about 384,400 km away and takes about 27 days to orbit Earth. The Moon causes ocean tides and helps stabilize Earth's rotation.

The Moon is the only place in space humans have visited - 12 astronauts walked on the Moon between 1969 and 1972!

🛡️ Protecting Earth

Earth has a magnetic field that protects us from harmful radiation from the Sun. It also has an atmosphere that burns up most space rocks before they hit the ground!

We need to take care of our planet by keeping it clean and protecting nature!
