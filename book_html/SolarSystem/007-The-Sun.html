🌟 What is the Sun?

The Sun is a star - a huge ball of hot, glowing gas. It's not a planet! The Sun is at the center of our solar system and gives us light and heat.

Without the Sun, Earth would be freezing cold and dark. All living things need the Sun to survive!

🔥 How Hot is the Sun?

The Sun is incredibly hot! The surface is about 5,500°C (9,932°F) - that's super hot! But the inside is even hotter - about 15 million°C!

💡 Fun Fact

The Sun is so hot that you could melt metal on its surface!

📏 How Big is the Sun?

The Sun is HUGE! It's over 100 times bigger than Earth. In fact, over 1 million Earths could fit inside the Sun!

💡 Why is the Sun Important?

The Sun is very important for life on Earth:

  • It gives us light during the day
  • It keeps Earth warm enough for life
  • Plants use sunlight to make food (photosynthesis)
  • It gives us energy (solar power)
