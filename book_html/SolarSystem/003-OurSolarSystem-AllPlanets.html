🌟 Meet Our Solar System Family!

Our solar system has 8 planets that all orbit around the Sun. Each planet is unique and special in its own way!

☀️ Sun - Our star
🪨 Mercury - Smallest
🔥 Venus - Hottest
🌍 Earth - Our home
🔴 Mars - The red planet
🟠 Jupiter - Biggest
💍 Saturn - Has rings
🔵 Uranus - Ice giant
🔷 Neptune - Windy
