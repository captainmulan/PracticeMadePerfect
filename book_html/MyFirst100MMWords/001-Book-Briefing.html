My First 100 Myanmar Words

Interactive words for kids growing up overseas
Keep your child connected to Myanmar — even while growing up overseas.

📖 About This Book

Welcome! This book helps children aged 4–10 learn their first 100 Myanmar words through pictures, native-style audio, mini-games, and short stories — without starting with difficult letter writing.

Each chapter bridges English and Myanmar: tap 🍎 Apple, hear ပန်းသီး, play a 30-second game, and earn a badge.

💬 Author's Message

"Hello! I'm Jimmy Cooper. Many overseas Myanmar families told me the same worry: 'My child is forgetting our language.' This book starts with hearing and speaking — the natural way children learn. Writing comes later. Let's keep your child's connection to Myanmar alive, one word at a time."

🎯 What You'll Learn

  • 10 word chapters: Family, Food, Animals, Colors, Numbers, Body, Home, School, Feelings, Festivals
  • English → Myanmar bridge for every word
  • Tap-to-hear pronunciation practice
  • Catch-and-learn mini-games with badges
  • Parent phrase cards for dinner-table practice

🛤 Learning Path

👂 Hear → 👀 Recognize picture → 🗣 Speak → 📖 Read → ✍ Write

Why not writing first? Myanmar letters are hard. Overseas kids often cannot speak or recognize letters yet — and lose interest quickly. Words first keeps them engaged.
