📖 Story

Aye lives in Singapore with her family. Every evening, Grandma calls from Yangon. Aye learns to say each family word — so she can tell Grandma about everyone at home.
💡 In Myanmar, children often live with grandparents. Learning family words helps kids talk to relatives on video calls!
