🌟 The Ocean Zones

The ocean is divided into different zones based on how deep they are. Each zone has different light, temperature, and amazing creatures that live there!

Click on each zone in the ocean display above to learn more about it!

☀️ Sunlight Zone

The top layer of the ocean where sunlight reaches. This is where most sea life lives! You'll find colorful fish, coral reefs, and sea turtles here.

Depth: 0 to 200 meters

🌅 Twilight Zone

Also called the mesopelagic zone. Very little sunlight reaches here, so it's always dim. Creatures here have big eyes to see in the dark!

Depth: 200 to 1,000 meters

🌙 Midnight Zone

No sunlight reaches this zone at all! It's completely dark. Creatures here make their own light (bioluminescence) to see and communicate.

Depth: 1,000 to 4,000 meters

🕳️ Abyss Zone

The deepest part of the ocean! It's extremely cold and the pressure is very high. Strange and amazing creatures live here that we're still discovering!

Depth: 4,000 meters and deeper
