📚 Marine Mammals Explained

Learn more about ocean mammals!

🌡️ Warm-Blooded Ocean Dwellers

Unlike fish, marine mammals are warm-blooded! This means they keep their body temperature constant, even in cold ocean water.

They have thick layers of fat called blubber under their skin to keep them warm. Blubber also stores energy and helps them float!

💡 Fun Fact

Some whales have blubber up to 30 centimeters (12 inches) thick!

🫁 Breathing at the Surface

Marine mammals have lungs and must come to the surface to breathe. They have a special blowhole on top of their head that they use to breathe.

When they exhale, the warm air from their lungs hits the cold air and creates a spout of water vapor - that's the "blow" you see from whales!

🔊 Echolocation

Toothed whales and dolphins use echolocation to find food and navigate. They make clicking sounds that bounce off objects and return as echoes.

By listening to these echoes, they can tell what's around them, even in dark or murky water! It's like having built-in sonar!

🐋 Baleen vs. Toothed Whales

Whales are divided into two main groups:

  • Baleen whales - Have baleen plates instead of teeth. They filter huge amounts of water to catch tiny krill and plankton. Examples: blue whale, humpback whale.
  • Toothed whales - Have teeth and hunt fish and squid. They use echolocation to find prey. Examples: sperm whale, orca, dolphins.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Social Behavior

Many marine mammals are very social! Dolphins live in pods and work together to hunt and protect each other. Whales communicate with complex songs that can travel for miles.

Some marine mammals, like orcas, stay with their families for their entire lives!

🦭 Pinnipeds - Seals and Sea Lions

Pinnipeds are "fin-footed" marine mammals. They spend time both in water and on land.

  • True seals - No external ear flaps, move on land by wiggling on their bellies
  • Eared seals - Have external ear flaps, can walk on land using their front flippers
  • Walruses - Have long tusks and thick whiskers for finding food on the ocean floor
