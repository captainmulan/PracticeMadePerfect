Hello World - Explained

CONCEPT 1

📜 What is a Sequence?

A sequence is just a fancy word for "the order things happen in." When you make a sandwich, you do steps in order:

1

Get two pieces of bread

2

Add the filling

3

Put the bread together

If you did step 3 before step 1, it wouldn't work! Computers are the same - they follow instructions in the order you write them, from top to bottom.

CONCEPT 2

💻 Same Words, Different Order

Here's something surprising: using the exact same instructions but in a different order gives you a totally different result!

console.log("Hello"); console.log("World");
✅ Correct Order
🔀 Swapped Order
CONCEPT 3

⚙️ How the Computer Reads Your Program

The computer reads your program like a story - one line, then the next line, then the next - and it never skips ahead or peeks at later lines first.

1

Read line 1 and do exactly what it says.

2

Move to line 2 and do exactly what it says.

3

Keep going until there are no more lines left.

This is why programmers say a program "runs from top to bottom" - and why getting the sequence right matters so much!
