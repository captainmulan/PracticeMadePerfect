How Whack-a-Mole Works

Let's see what happens inside the computer!

💻 Our Program

hole.addEventListener("click", () => { if (hole.classList.contains("up")) { score++; } });
1

🧠 The Computer Waits

The computer doesn't do anything at first. It just quietly waits and listens for something to happen.

⬇️
2

🐹 A Mole Pops Up

Every second or so, a random hole shows its mole. This happens on its own, without you clicking anything.

⬇️
3

👆 You Click!

The moment you click a hole, that's an event - something just happened!

⬇️
4

🔎 The Computer Checks

"Was a mole actually up in that hole?" If yes - point scored! If not, nothing happens.

⬇️
5

🏆 The Score Updates

The screen instantly shows your new score.
