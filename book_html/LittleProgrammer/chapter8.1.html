Chapter 8.1 — Final Project Ideas

Pick one of these mini-projects and try to build it by combining chapters:

  • Star Collector: Use movement (chapters 2 & 4), conditions (chapter 5), and variables (chapter 6) to collect stars and count score.
  • Interactive Story: Use events (chapter 3) and branching (chapter 5) to choose story paths.
  • Remix: Try a Scratch starter project and add your own characters and rules (see Scratch Ideas).
