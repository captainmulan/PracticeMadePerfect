What is a Computer?

🤔 Let's Learn!

A computer is a machine that follows instructions! It takes information (input), thinks about it (process), and gives you something back (output).

You're using a computer right now to read this! 🎉

🔧 Parts of a Computer

Computers have many parts that work together:

⌨️ Keyboard
🖥️ Monitor
🖱️ Mouse
🎧 Speaker
🌟 Fun Fact!
The first computers were as big as a whole room! Now we have tiny computers in our watches!
