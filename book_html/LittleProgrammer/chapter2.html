Chapter 2 What is a Program?

A program is a sequence of instructions that the computer follows. Programs can be simple (move a character) or complex (manage a whole game).

Key ideas

  • Programs are made of steps (instructions)
  • Programs can repeat steps, make decisions, and store values
  • We can test programs by trying them and fixing mistakes
