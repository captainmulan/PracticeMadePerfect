📖 About This Book

Little Programmer is an interactive, game-based introduction to coding for young learners. Instead of reading about programming, kids play their way through it - tapping, clicking, and building tiny programs to solve fun puzzles and games.

Each chapter pairs one big programming idea (like commands, events, variables, or loops) with a hands-on mini-game, a friendly explainer, and a short quiz to check understanding - building real coding instincts before kids ever type a line of code.

🚀 How to Use This Book

1

Open a chapter and read the short, friendly explanation of the idea.

2

Play the mini-game - it's built entirely around that chapter's concept.

3

Dig deeper with the "Explained" page and its interactive demo.

4

Take the short quiz to check understanding before moving on.

🌟 For parents & teachers: no prior coding knowledge is required - either for the child or for you! Every concept is introduced from scratch, in plain language, with lots of visual feedback.
