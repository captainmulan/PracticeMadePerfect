🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the lungs above to make them breathe! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about your amazing lungs.

Your lungs help you breathe by taking in oxygen and removing carbon dioxide from your body!

💨 Quick Facts

  • You breathe about 20,000 times a day
  • Your lungs are the only organs that can float on water
  • Your right lung is bigger than your left lung
  • Your lungs are protected by your rib cage
  • Your lungs have tiny air sacs called alveoli
