🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the heart above to make it beat! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about your amazing heart.

Your heart is an incredible muscle that works hard every day to keep you alive and healthy!

❤️ Quick Facts

  • Your heart beats about 100,000 times a day
  • Your heart is about the size of your fist
  • Your heart pumps blood through your body
  • Your heart has 4 chambers
  • Your heart never stops working!
