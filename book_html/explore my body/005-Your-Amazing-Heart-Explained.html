📖 Your Amazing Heart Explained

Learn more about your incredible heart!

❤️ What Is Your Heart?

Your heart is a strong muscle that pumps blood throughout your body! It's about the size of your fist and sits in the middle of your chest, slightly to the left.

Your heart works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, even when you're sleeping! It never takes a break because your body always needs blood to survive.

💡 Fun Fact

Your heart beats about 100,000 times every single day!

🏠 The 4 Chambers of Your Heart

Your heart has 4 special rooms called chambers:

  • Right Atrium - Receives blood from your body
  • Right Ventricle - Pumps blood to your lungs
  • Left Atrium - Receives blood from your lungs
  • Left Ventricle - Pumps blood to your body

🩸 What Does Your Heart Do?

Your heart pumps blood through a network of tubes called blood vessels. This blood carries oxygen and nutrients to every part of your body!

After delivering oxygen, the blood returns to your heart to get fresh oxygen from your lungs, and the cycle starts all over again.

💓 How Your Heart Beats

Your heart beats in a special rhythm. First, the top chambers fill with blood. Then they squeeze to push blood to the bottom chambers. Finally, the bottom chambers squeeze to pump blood out to your lungs and body.

💡 Fun Fact

Your heart pumps about 5 liters of blood every minute - that's like 10 bottles of water!

🏃 Keeping Your Heart Healthy

You can help keep your heart strong and healthy by:

  • Exercising and playing sports
  • Eating healthy foods like fruits and vegetables
  • Getting enough sleep
  • Not smoking (when you're older)
  • Managing stress
