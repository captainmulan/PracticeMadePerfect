Explore My Body

An Amazing Journey Inside You!

📖 About This Book

Welcome to Explore My Body! This interactive ebook will take you on an amazing journey inside your own body to learn about how it works and what makes you special.

You'll discover your heart, brain, bones, muscles, and more! You'll meet Dr. Body, a friendly doctor who will help you learn through fun activities and quizzes.

📚 Book Details

  • Target Age: 7-8 years old
  • Total Chapters: 41 chapters
  • Format: Interactive HTML ebook
  • Features: Body part activities, matching games, drag-drop quizzes, and Olympic-style podium celebrations!

🎯 What You'll Learn

  • Your amazing heart and how it beats
  • Your brain and how you think
  • Your bones and muscles
  • How your lungs help you breathe
  • Your stomach and digestion
  • Your skin and senses

🎮 How to Use This Book

  • Start with the Index to see all chapters
  • Choose your character before starting quizzes
  • Interact with body parts in activities
  • Learn how your body works in explained chapters
  • Complete quizzes after each chapter
  • Win medals on the podium after each quiz!
