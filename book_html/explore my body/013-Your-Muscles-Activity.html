Your muscles help you move, lift things, and even keep your heart beating!

🏋️ Quick Facts

  • You have over 600 muscles in your body
  • Your muscles work in pairs to move your body
  • Your strongest muscle is your jaw
  • Your muscles make up about 40% of your body weight
  • Your heart is a muscle that never rests
