📖 Your Muscles Explained

Learn more about your amazing muscles!

💪 What Are Your Muscles?

Your muscles are special tissues that help you move! They pull on your bones to make your body move in different ways.

You have over 600 muscles in your body, and they work together to help you walk, run, jump, and even smile!

💡 Fun Fact

Your muscles make up about 40% of your body weight - that's almost half of you!

🏋️ How Muscles Work

Your muscles work in pairs! When one muscle pulls (contracts), the other muscle relaxes. This is how you can move your arms and legs back and forth.

Muscles are attached to your bones by tough bands called tendons. When a muscle contracts, it pulls on the tendon, which pulls on the bone and makes it move!

🎯 Types of Muscles

Your body has three main types of muscles:

  • Skeletal muscles - Attached to bones, help you move (like arm muscles)
  • Smooth muscles - In your organs, work automatically (like in your stomach)
  • Cardiac muscle - Your heart muscle, never stops beating

💪 Keeping Your Muscles Strong

You can help keep your muscles strong and healthy by:

  • Playing sports and being active
  • Eating protein-rich foods like meat and beans
  • Getting enough sleep
  • Drinking plenty of water
  • Stretching before and after exercise

💡 Fun Fact

Your strongest muscle is your jaw - it can bite with great force to chew tough food!
