🎯 Activity Instructions

Tap on the bone above to shake it! Each time you tap it, you'll learn a new fun fact about your amazing bones.

Your bones are the framework that holds your body together and protects your important organs!

💪 Quick Facts

  • You have 206 bones in your body
  • Your bones are alive and growing
  • Your bones protect your organs
  • Your bones make blood cells
  • Your bones get stronger with exercise
