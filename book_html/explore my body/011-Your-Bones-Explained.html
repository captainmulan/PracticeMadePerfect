📖 Your Bones Explained

Learn more about your amazing bones!

🦴 What Are Your Bones?

Your bones are the hard parts inside your body that give you shape and support! They work like a frame that holds everything together.

Your bones are alive - they have blood vessels and nerves inside them. They can grow and repair themselves if they get hurt!

💡 Fun Fact

You have 206 bones in your body as an adult, but babies are born with about 270 bones!

🏗️ What Do Your Bones Do?

Your bones have many important jobs:

  • Support - They hold your body up and give you shape
  • Protection - They protect your organs (like your skull protects your brain)
  • Movement - They work with muscles to help you move
  • Blood Making - Bone marrow makes blood cells
  • Storage - They store minerals like calcium

🦴 Types of Bones

Your body has different types of bones:

  • Long bones - Like your arm and leg bones
  • Short bones - Like the bones in your wrists and ankles
  • Flat bones - Like your ribs and skull bones
  • Irregular bones - Like your vertebrae (spine bones)

💪 Keeping Your Bones Strong

You can help keep your bones strong and healthy by:

  • Drinking milk and eating dairy products
  • Eating foods with calcium like cheese and yogurt
  • Playing sports and being active
  • Getting enough vitamin D from sunshine
  • Wearing safety gear like helmets

💡 Fun Fact

The longest bone in your body is the femur (thigh bone) - it's about 1/4 of your height!
